How it all started.

It started as a little project in Thessaloniki,Greece.As a bike shop primarily and educating people about the benefits of using bikes more for their everyday commute.Today we are one of the leading bicycle retailers in EU and #6 in US

Our Vision

To someday lower the carbon dioxide footprint of the planet by doing our small part,one step at a time,minus one car at a time and plus one bicycle at a time.

Why everythingbicycle.com?

everythingbicycle.com is a rapidly growing business especially at ages 18-40. Soon we will be organising events and big bike-marathons all over the country of Greece first,then the rest of Europe.

We are adding more and more options for every budget,prices will range from 120€ all the way to 10.000€! Our bikes have been drive-tested in the hardest conditions possible,by our suppliers,clients and by us of course, so we assure that you are being provided with some of the best gear ever created.
